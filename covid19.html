Acknowledgement of Country

contour 556 acknowledges the Ngunnawal people, the traditional custodians of the land on which we live, work, rest and play. We pay our respects to Elders past, present and emerging and value their cultures, identities, and continuing connection to country, water, kin and community. Sovereginty was never ceded; this always was, always will be, Aboriginal land.
